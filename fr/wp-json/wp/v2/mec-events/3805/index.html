With Terry LePage of the Deep Adaptation Forum<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Sample a conversation with Deep Adaptation Forum facilitators… we will honestly speak about our responses to ecological or social collapse that is anticipated or under way. Share your thoughts and feelings in this nonjudgemental, non-prescriptive space. Be part of our mission, “Embodying and enabling loving responses to our predicament.” You are welcome to drop in and depart as you wish- please do so gently.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
