With Robina McCurdy of Earthcare Education Aotearoa<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Permaculture design with an emphasis on the functional and subtle edges between nature restoration and human habitat – where the needs of the land and people are reciprocally met. It is practically applied Deep Ecology. Robina will show a range of examples of her work throughout the world which embody this synthesis of landscape and social design. The ultimate expression of her approach is in conscious eco-village design, from contemporary purpose-built European eco-villages, to retrofitting squatter settlements into places of dignity. This session is about designing for Belonging in the deepest sense possible.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
